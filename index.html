Mattel Set To Release Its New Fast & Furious NFT Series

Mattel is set to launch the newest edition of its Hot Wheels NFT Garage Series on May 22.

The new NFT series is based on the iconic series Fast and Furious and is timed alongside the release of the latest installment in the series.

The Hot Wheels NFT Garage x Fast & Furious collection will be based on the Flow Blockchain.

It will feature cars from the movies, including Dominic Toretto's 1970 Dodge Charger, Brian O'Conner's 1969 Camero, and Suki's Honda S2000 from 2 Fast 2 Furious.

The cost of the limited edition NFTs will be $20 per pack.

Mattel has been experimenting with ‘Digital Toys’ NFTs and is also set to launch a collection of Star Wars-themed digital collectibles called Cryptoys.

Another NFT collection by Mattel, Boss Beauties x Barbie, will be released on May 18, 2023, that will pay homage to the 250 careers of Barbie.
